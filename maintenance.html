Manual Starting Backups

Manual execute a backup.¶
 cd  provisioning
 ansible-playbook -i inventories/prod maintenance/playbook-execute-backup.yml --extra-vars "backup_destination=backblaze"

Basic lookup. Fails if backblaze secrects doesn’t exist

module:set_fact
  • B2_ACCOUNT_ID={{ lookup('passwordstore', password_store_paths.B2_ACCOUNT_ID_PATH ,convert_data=False) |string}}
  • RESTIC_REPO_PASSWORD={{ lookup('passwordstore', password_store_paths.RESTIC_REPO_PASSWORD_PATH ,convert_data=False) |string}}
  • B2_ACCOUNT_KEY={{ lookup('passwordstore', password_store_paths.B2_ACCOUNT_KEY_PATH ,convert_data=False) | b64encode |string}}
condition:backup_destination is defined and backup_destination == ‘backblaze’
host:localhost
